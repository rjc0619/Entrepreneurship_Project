Save the Princess

September 2018 (2 weeks)

In the game, Save the Princess, your obcective is to rescue the princess. But first you need to go through all of the obstacles blocking your way to the princess. In each level you will fight enemies that are prventing you from getting to the princess. Each level will decrease the number of enemies but increase the health of the enemies making the game harder. In the gave you will be controling a peasant unsing its bow to shoot the enemies. If you successfully kill all the enemies from hitting them mutiple times, you will proceed onto the next level. Every single time you touch the enemies will result in a deduction in health. There is healing in levels where you need to touch the heart to increse your health.
