Save the Princess

Objective: Create a mutistage interactive game

September 2018 (2 weeks)

In the game, Save the Princess, your obective is to rescue the princess. But first you need to go through all of the obstacles blocking your way to the princess. In each level you will fight enemies that are prventing you from getting to the princess. Each level will decrease the number of enemies but increase the health of the enemies making the game harder. In the gave you will be controling a peasant unsing its bow to shoot the enemies. If you successfully kill all the enemies from hitting them mutiple times, you will proceed onto the next level. Every single time you touch the enemies will result in a deduction in health. There is healing in levels where you need to touch the heart to increse your health. In this project my partner and I thought of many ideas to make a game. It took some time but we came down to one idea. My partner mainly worked on the code but whenever he felt like he was stuck or that he could not figure something out then I would help him. Most of the time I would work on the documentations and if I was done with the documentations then I would watch my partner code and guide him to code the project. During the progress of creating the game we were able to seek help form our nehboring groups. We were able to help them when they need help and they helped us when we needed help. Finally, we were able to complete the final version of the game that is show above.
