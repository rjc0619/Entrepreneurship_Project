The Problem

We all know and are aware of what the world famous water bottle is: hydro flask. This water bottle is able to withstand the temperature of cool beverages for up to twenty-four hours and warm beverages no more than twelve. A design like this is beneficial to many, however, has a downside to the cost. The cost of hydro flasks ranges from $40-$60. This is the problem we are facing because we want to expand the number of people who want to drink temperature insulated beverages. Our mission is to bring this opportunity to everyone in a more cost-efficient and inclusive way. We mainly want to reach out to any age audience (specifically teens to young adults) and people involved in athletics or any audiences who require cold or warm beverages. A solution is needed to this problem because drinking beverages based on the temperature you want is a simple thing and should be accessible to whoever wants it. When things are too expensive, it can be very discouraging. Although insulated reusable water bottles have their benefits to people and their environment, many people who don't have insulated water bottles will turn to cold plastic water bottles that people just put in the fridge. Many water bottles are not disposed of in the correct disposal causing most water bottles to end up in landfill or even in the ocean. When water bottles end up in the ocean most sea creature will mistake the water bottle with food. Later on, they find out that what they just consumed is indigestible and die because it's too late to be saved.
