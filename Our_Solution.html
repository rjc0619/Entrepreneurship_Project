If you have a normal bottle and it is not heat insulated, then you can add the insuLiquid to keep the liquid inside a consistent temperature. Now! You won't have to worry about actually buying an expensive water bottle that is heat insulated. On the outer layer of the sleeve, we decided that it would be silicone.
